About Me

Others create content for pay, I get paid to create content. The difference? A deep-rooted passion to design and construct engaging deliverables that help your projects become more creative, effective and efficient.

As a NYC-based, multi-disciplinary content creator working across documentary videography, special effects, and digital photography, I've kindled a reputation for creating warm, content-focused design with a variety of clients 

My designs help businesses look more professional, make greater sales, and organically grow their audiences. During my time as a Video and Content Creator for The Actors Theatre Workshop, my videos increased viewership by almost 80 percent. 

My body of work has been featured in industry publications like the Joan B. Kroc Institute for Peace & Justice WorldLink Reader, and recognized by institutions such as The George Washington University. In 2019, I was selected to receive the University and Alumni Award. 

By blending innovation, creativity, and an expert eye for photography, I create powerful products that bring your story to life!
